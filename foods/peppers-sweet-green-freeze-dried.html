Pros and Cons of Peppers, sweet, green, freeze-dried
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat and Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Protein, Niacin, Folate, Iron, Magnesium and Phosphorus, and a very good source of Dietary Fiber, Vitamin A, Vitamin C, Vitamin K, Thiamin, Riboflavin, Vitamin B6, Potassium, Copper and Manganese.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
