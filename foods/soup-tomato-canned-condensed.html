Pros and Cons of Soup, tomato, canned, condensed
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Saturated Fat, and very low in Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Vitamin A, Iron, Potassium, Copper and Selenium, and a very good source of Vitamin C.

The bad: This food is very high in Sodium, and a large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
