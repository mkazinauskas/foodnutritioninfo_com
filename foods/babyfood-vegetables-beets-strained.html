Pros and Cons of Babyfood, vegetables, beets, strained
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat and Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Vitamin C, Magnesium, Potassium and Copper, and a very good source of Dietary Fiber and Folate.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
