Pros and Cons of Babyfood, fruit, peaches, junior
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat, Cholesterol and Sodium. It is also a good source of Vitamin E (Alpha Tocopherol), Vitamin K and Niacin, and a very good source of Vitamin A and Vitamin C.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
