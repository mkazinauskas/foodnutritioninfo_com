Pros and Cons of Cereals ready-to-eat, KRAFT, POST Raisin Bran Cereal
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat and Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Dietary Fiber, Magnesium, Phosphorus and Zinc, and a very good source of Thiamin, Riboflavin, Niacin, Vitamin B6, Folate, Vitamin B12, Iron and Manganese.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
