Pros and Cons of Soymilk, chocolate, unfortified
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Saturated Fat, and very low in Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Vitamin B12, Copper and Selenium, and a very good source of Riboflavin.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
