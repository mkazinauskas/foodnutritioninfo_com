Pros and Cons of Soy flour, low-fat
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Saturated Fat, and very low in Cholesterol and Sodium. It is also a good source of Dietary Fiber, Protein, Thiamin, Vitamin B6, Folate, Iron, Magnesium, Phosphorus and Potassium, and a very good source of Copper, Manganese and Selenium.
