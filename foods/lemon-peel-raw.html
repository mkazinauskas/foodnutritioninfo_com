Pros and Cons of Lemon peel, raw
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat, Cholesterol and Sodium. It is also a good source of Vitamin B6, and a very good source of Dietary Fiber, Vitamin C and Calcium.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
