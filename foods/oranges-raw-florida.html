Pros and Cons of Oranges, raw, Florida
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat, Cholesterol and Sodium. It is also a good source of Thiamin and Potassium, and a very good source of Dietary Fiber and Vitamin C.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
