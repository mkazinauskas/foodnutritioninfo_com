Pros and Cons of Rowal, raw
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Saturated Fat, and very low in Sodium. It is also a good source of Iron, and a very good source of Dietary Fiber, Vitamin C and Copper.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
