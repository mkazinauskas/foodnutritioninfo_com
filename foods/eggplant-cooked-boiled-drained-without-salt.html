Pros and Cons of Eggplant, cooked, boiled, drained, without salt
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat, Cholesterol and Sodium. It is also a good source of Vitamin K, Thiamin, Vitamin B6 and Manganese, and a very good source of Dietary Fiber.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
