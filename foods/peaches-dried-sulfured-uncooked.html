Pros and Cons of Peaches, dried, sulfured, uncooked
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat, Cholesterol and Sodium. It is also a good source of Dietary Fiber, Vitamin A and Potassium.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
