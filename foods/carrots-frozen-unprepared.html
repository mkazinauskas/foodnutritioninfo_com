Pros and Cons of Carrots, frozen, unprepared
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat and Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Vitamin C, Vitamin B6, Potassium and Copper, and a very good source of Dietary Fiber, Vitamin A, Vitamin K and Manganese.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
