Pros and Cons of Okra, cooked, boiled, drained, with salt
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Saturated Fat, and very low in Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Protein, Riboflavin, Niacin, Phosphorus, Potassium, Zinc and Copper, and a very good source of Dietary Fiber, Vitamin A, Vitamin C, Vitamin K, Thiamin, Vitamin B6, Folate, Calcium, Magnesium and Manganese.

The bad: This food is very high in Sodium, and a large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
