Pros and Cons of Pumpkin, canned, with salt
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Saturated Fat, and very low in Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Vitamin E (Alpha Tocopherol), Pantothenic Acid, Magnesium, Phosphorus, Potassium and Copper, and a very good source of Dietary Fiber, Vitamin A, Vitamin C, Vitamin K, Iron and Manganese.

The bad: This food is very high in Sodium, and a large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
