Pros and Cons of SILK Light Plain, soymilk
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat and Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Vitamin A, Magnesium, Potassium and Selenium, and a very good source of Vitamin D, Riboflavin, Vitamin B12 and Calcium.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
