Pros and Cons of Acerola juice, raw
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Saturated Fat, and very low in Cholesterol and Sodium. It is also a good source of Riboflavin, Folate, Iron, Magnesium, Potassium and Copper, and a very good source of Vitamin A and Vitamin C.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
