Pros and Cons of Pickles, cucumber, sour
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Saturated Fat, and very low in Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Vitamin A and Iron, and a very good source of Dietary Fiber, Vitamin K and Copper.

The bad: This food is very high in Sodium, and a large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
