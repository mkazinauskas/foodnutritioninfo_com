Pros and Cons of Milk, dry, nonfat, instant, with added vitamin A
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Cholesterol, and very low in Saturated Fat. It is also a good source of Vitamin A, Vitamin B12, Potassium and Selenium, and a very good source of Protein, Vitamin D, Riboflavin, Calcium and Phosphorus.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
