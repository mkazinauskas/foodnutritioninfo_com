Pros and Cons of Blackberry juice, canned
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat, Cholesterol and Sodium. It is also a good source of Vitamin E (Alpha Tocopherol), Magnesium and Copper, and a very good source of Vitamin C and Vitamin K.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
