Pros and Cons of Milk, canned, evaporated, nonfat
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Saturated Fat and Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Potassium, and a very good source of Protein, Vitamin D, Riboflavin, Calcium and Phosphorus.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
