Pros and Cons of Jerusalem-artichokes, raw
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat, Cholesterol and Sodium. It is also a good source of Thiamin, Phosphorus and Potassium, and a very good source of Iron.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.

Glycemic load of Jerusalem-artichokes, raw is: 7

Glycemic load is a way of expressing a food or meal's effect on blood-sugar levels. A typical target for total Estimated Glycemic Load is 100 or less per day.
