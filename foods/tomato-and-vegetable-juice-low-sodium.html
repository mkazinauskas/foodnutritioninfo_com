Pros and Cons of Tomato and vegetable juice, low sodium
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat and Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Dietary Fiber, Thiamin, Niacin, Iron and Magnesium, and a very good source of Vitamin A, Vitamin C, Vitamin K, Vitamin B6, Folate, Potassium and Copper.

The bad: This food is high in Sodium, and a large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
