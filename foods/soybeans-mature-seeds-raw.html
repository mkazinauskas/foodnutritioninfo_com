Pros and Cons of Soybeans, mature seeds, raw
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Cholesterol and Sodium. It is also a good source of Vitamin K, Thiamin, Riboflavin, Iron, Magnesium, Phosphorus, Potassium and Copper, and a very good source of Protein, Folate and Manganese.
