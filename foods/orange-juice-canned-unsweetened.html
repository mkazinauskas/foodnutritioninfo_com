Completeness score of Orange juice, canned, unsweetened is 38.

A Completeness Score between 0 and 100 is a relative indication of how complete the food is with respect to these nutrients. Protein quality for Orange juice, canned, unsweetened
100 grams

Protein quality score of Orange juice, canned, unsweetened is 25.

An Amino Acid Score of 100 or higher indicates a complete or high-quality protein. If the Amino Acid Score is less than 100, a link is provided to complementary sources of protein. By combining complementary proteins, you may be able to increase the overall quality of the protein you consume.

Pros and Cons of Orange juice, canned, unsweetened
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat, Cholesterol and Sodium. It is also a good source of Folate and Potassium, and a very good source of Vitamin C.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.

Glycemic load of Orange juice, canned, unsweetened is: 4

Glycemic load is a way of expressing a food or meal's effect on blood-sugar levels. A typical target for total Estimated Glycemic Load is 100 or less per day.
