Pros and Cons of Onions, frozen, whole, unprepared
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Sodium, and very low in Saturated Fat and Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Dietary Fiber, Vitamin B6, Folate, Calcium, Potassium and Manganese, and a very good source of Vitamin C.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
