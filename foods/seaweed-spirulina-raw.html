Pros and Cons of Seaweed, spirulina, raw
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Pantothenic Acid, Magnesium and Potassium, and a very good source of Protein, Thiamin, Riboflavin, Niacin, Iron, Copper and Manganese.

The bad: This food is high in Sodium.

Glycemic load of Seaweed, spirulina, raw is: 2

Glycemic load is a way of expressing a food or meal's effect on blood-sugar levels. A typical target for total Estimated Glycemic Load is 100 or less per day.
