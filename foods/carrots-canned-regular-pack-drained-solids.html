Pros and Cons of Carrots, canned, regular pack, drained solids
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat and Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Vitamin C, Vitamin E (Alpha Tocopherol), Niacin and Iron, and a very good source of Dietary Fiber, Vitamin A, Vitamin K, Vitamin B6, Potassium, Copper and Manganese.

The bad: This food is very high in Sodium, and a large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
