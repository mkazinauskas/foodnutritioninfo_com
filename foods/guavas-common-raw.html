Pros and Cons of Guavas, common, raw
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Saturated Fat, and very low in Cholesterol and Sodium. It is also a good source of Vitamin A, Folate, Potassium, Copper and Manganese, and a very good source of Dietary Fiber and Vitamin C.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
