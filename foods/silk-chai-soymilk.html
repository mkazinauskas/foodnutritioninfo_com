Pros and Cons of SILK Chai, soymilk
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Saturated Fat, and very low in Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Vitamin D, Riboflavin and Vitamin B12, and a very good source of Calcium.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
