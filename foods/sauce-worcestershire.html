Pros and Cons of Sauce, worcestershire
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat and Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Calcium and Copper, and a very good source of Vitamin C, Iron and Potassium.

The bad: This food is very high in Sodium, and a large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
