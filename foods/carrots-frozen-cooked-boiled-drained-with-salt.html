Pros and Cons of Carrots, frozen, cooked, boiled, drained, with salt
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Saturated Fat, and very low in Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Vitamin C, Vitamin E (Alpha Tocopherol), Vitamin B6, Potassium and Copper, and a very good source of Dietary Fiber, Vitamin A, Vitamin K and Manganese.

The bad: This food is very high in Sodium, and a large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
