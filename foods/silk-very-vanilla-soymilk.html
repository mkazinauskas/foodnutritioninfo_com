Pros and Cons of SILK Very Vanilla, soymilk
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Saturated Fat, and very low in Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Vitamin A, and a very good source of Vitamin C, Vitamin D, Riboflavin, Vitamin B12 and Calcium.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
