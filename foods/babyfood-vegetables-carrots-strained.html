Pros and Cons of Babyfood, vegetables, carrots, strained
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat and Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Vitamin B6 and Folate, and a very good source of Dietary Fiber, Vitamin A, Vitamin C, Vitamin K and Potassium.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
