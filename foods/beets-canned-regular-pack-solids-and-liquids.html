Pros and Cons of Beets, canned, regular pack, solids and liquids
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat and Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Dietary Fiber, Vitamin C, Iron, Magnesium and Potassium, and a very good source of Folate and Manganese.

The bad: This food is high in Sodium, and a large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
