Pros and Cons of Babyfood, vegetables, squash, strained
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Sodium, and very low in Saturated Fat and Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Dietary Fiber, Niacin, Vitamin B6, Magnesium, Potassium and Manganese, and a very good source of Vitamin A and Vitamin K.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
