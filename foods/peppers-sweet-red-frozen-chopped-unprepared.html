Pros and Cons of Peppers, sweet, red, frozen, chopped, unprepared
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Saturated Fat and Sodium, and very low in Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Riboflavin, Folate, Iron, Potassium and Copper, and a very good source of Dietary Fiber, Vitamin A, Vitamin C, Vitamin E (Alpha Tocopherol), Vitamin K, Thiamin, Niacin, Vitamin B6 and Manganese.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
