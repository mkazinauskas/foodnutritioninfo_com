Pros and Cons of Tomato products, canned, sauce, with mushrooms
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat and Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Dietary Fiber, Vitamin K, Thiamin, Riboflavin, Niacin, Vitamin B6, Pantothenic Acid, Iron and Magnesium, and a very good source of Vitamin A, Vitamin C, Vitamin E (Alpha Tocopherol), Potassium, Copper and Manganese.

The bad: This food is very high in Sodium, and a large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
