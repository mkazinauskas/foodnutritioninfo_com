Pros and Cons of Bamboo shoots, canned, drained solids
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Sodium, and very low in Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Protein, Vitamin E (Alpha Tocopherol), Phosphorus and Potassium, and a very good source of Dietary Fiber, Vitamin B6, Zinc, Copper and Manganese.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
