Pros and Cons of Babyfood, carrots, toddler
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Saturated Fat, and very low in Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Vitamin E (Alpha Tocopherol), Potassium and Copper, and a very good source of Dietary Fiber, Vitamin A and Vitamin K.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
