Pros and Cons of Pickles, cucumber, dill, low sodium
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Saturated Fat, and very low in Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Vitamin C, Iron, Magnesium, Phosphorus, Potassium and Copper, and a very good source of Dietary Fiber, Vitamin A and Vitamin K.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
