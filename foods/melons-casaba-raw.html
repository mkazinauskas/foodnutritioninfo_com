Pros and Cons of Melons, casaba, raw
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Sodium, and very low in Saturated Fat and Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Dietary Fiber, Vitamin K, Potassium and Copper, and a very good source of Vitamin C and Vitamin B6.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
