Pros and Cons of Melon, banana (Navajo)
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Sodium. It is also a good source of Niacin, Vitamin B6, Magnesium and Potassium, and a very good source of Vitamin C, Vitamin K and Folate.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
