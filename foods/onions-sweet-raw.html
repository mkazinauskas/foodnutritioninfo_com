Pros and Cons of Onions, sweet, raw
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Sodium, and very low in Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Dietary Fiber, Folate, Potassium and Manganese, and a very good source of Vitamin C and Vitamin B6.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.

Glycemic load of Onions, sweet, raw is: 2

Glycemic load is a way of expressing a food or meal's effect on blood-sugar levels. A typical target for total Estimated Glycemic Load is 100 or less per day.
