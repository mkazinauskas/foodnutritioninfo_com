Pros and Cons of Strawberries, frozen, sweetened, sliced
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat, Cholesterol and Sodium. It is also a good source of Manganese, and a very good source of Vitamin C.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
