Pros and Cons of Cheese substitute, mozzarella
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Protein, Vitamin A, Riboflavin and Selenium, and a very good source of Calcium and Phosphorus.

The bad: This food is high in Sodium, and a large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
