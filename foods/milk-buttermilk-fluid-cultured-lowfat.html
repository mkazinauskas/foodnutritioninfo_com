Pros and Cons of Milk, buttermilk, fluid, cultured, lowfat
100 grams

The good: This food is a good source of Potassium, and a very good source of Protein, Riboflavin, Calcium and Phosphorus.

The bad: This food is high in Sodium, and a large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
