Pros and Cons of Orange juice drink
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat, Cholesterol and Sodium. It is also a very good source of Vitamin C, Thiamin, Riboflavin, Niacin and Vitamin B6.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
