Nutrient balance indicator for Soy protein isolate

Completeness score of Soy protein isolate is 44.

A Completeness Score between 0 and 100 is a relative indication of how complete the food is with respect to these nutrients. Protein quality for Soy protein isolate
100 grams

Protein quality score of Soy protein isolate is 108.

An Amino Acid Score of 100 or higher indicates a complete or high-quality protein. If the Amino Acid Score is less than 100, a link is provided to complementary sources of protein. By combining complementary proteins, you may be able to increase the overall quality of the protein you consume.

Pros and Cons of Soy protein isolate
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat and Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Folate, and a very good source of Protein, Iron, Phosphorus, Copper and Manganese.

The bad: This food is high in Sodium.

Glycemic load of Soy protein isolate is: 2

Glycemic load is a way of expressing a food or meal's effect on blood-sugar levels. A typical target for total Estimated Glycemic Load is 100 or less per day.
