Pros and Cons of Grape juice drink, canned
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat, Cholesterol and Sodium. It is also a good source of Manganese, and a very good source of Vitamin C, Thiamin and Riboflavin.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
