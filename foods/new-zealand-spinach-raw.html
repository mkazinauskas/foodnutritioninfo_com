Pros and Cons of New Zealand spinach, raw
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Saturated Fat, and very low in Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Thiamin, Niacin, Folate, Pantothenic Acid, Phosphorus, Potassium and Zinc, and a very good source of Vitamin A, Vitamin C, Riboflavin, Vitamin B6, Calcium, Iron, Magnesium, Copper and Manganese.

The bad: This food is very high in Sodium.
