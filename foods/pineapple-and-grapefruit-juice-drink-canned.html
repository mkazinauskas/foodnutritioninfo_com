Pros and Cons of Pineapple and grapefruit juice drink, canned
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Sodium, and very low in Saturated Fat and Cholesterol. It is also a very good source of Vitamin C and Manganese.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
