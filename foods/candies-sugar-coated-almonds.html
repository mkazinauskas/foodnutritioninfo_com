Pros and Cons of Candies, sugar-coated almonds
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Saturated Fat, and very low in Cholesterol and Sodium. It is also a good source of Vitamin E (Alpha Tocopherol) and Manganese.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
