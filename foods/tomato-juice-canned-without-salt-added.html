Pros and Cons of Tomato juice, canned, without salt added
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Sodium, and very low in Saturated Fat and Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Vitamin K, Thiamin, Niacin, Pantothenic Acid, Iron, Magnesium, Copper and Manganese, and a very good source of Vitamin A, Vitamin C, Vitamin B6, Folate and Potassium.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
