Pros and Cons of Eggplant, cooked, boiled, drained, with salt
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat and Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Vitamin K, Thiamin, Vitamin B6, Folate, Potassium and Manganese, and a very good source of Dietary Fiber.

The bad: This food is very high in Sodium, and a large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
