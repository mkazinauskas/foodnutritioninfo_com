Pros and Cons of Milk, buttermilk, dried
100 grams

The good: This food is a good source of Vitamin B12 and Potassium, and a very good source of Protein, Riboflavin, Calcium and Phosphorus.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
