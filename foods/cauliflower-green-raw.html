Pros and Cons of Cauliflower, green, raw
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Saturated Fat, and very low in Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Protein, Thiamin, Riboflavin, Niacin, Calcium, Iron, Magnesium, Phosphorus and Zinc, and a very good source of Dietary Fiber, Vitamin C, Vitamin K, Vitamin B6, Folate, Pantothenic Acid, Potassium and Manganese.

The bad: A large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.

Glycemic load of Cauliflower, green, raw is: 3

Glycemic load is a way of expressing a food or meal's effect on blood-sugar levels. A typical target for total Estimated Glycemic Load is 100 or less per day.
