Pros and Cons of Tomatoes, red, ripe, canned, packed in tomato juice
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat and Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Dietary Fiber, Vitamin A, Vitamin E (Alpha Tocopherol), Vitamin K, Thiamin, Riboflavin, Niacin, Calcium, Magnesium, Copper and Manganese, and a very good source of Vitamin C, Vitamin B6, Iron and Potassium.

The bad: This food is very high in Sodium, and a large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
