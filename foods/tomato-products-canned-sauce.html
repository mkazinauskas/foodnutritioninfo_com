Pros and Cons of Tomato products, canned, sauce
100 grams

The good: This food is very low in Saturated Fat and Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Vitamin K, Riboflavin, Vitamin B6, Folate, Pantothenic Acid, Magnesium and Phosphorus, and a very good source of Dietary Fiber, Vitamin A, Vitamin C, Vitamin E (Alpha Tocopherol), Niacin, Iron, Potassium, Copper and Manganese.

The bad: This food is very high in Sodium, and a large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.

Glycemic load of Tomato products, canned, sauce is: 2

Glycemic load is a way of expressing a food or meal's effect on blood-sugar levels. A typical target for total Estimated Glycemic Load is 100 or less per day.
