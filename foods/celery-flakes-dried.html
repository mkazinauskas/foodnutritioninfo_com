Pros and Cons of Celery flakes, dried
100 grams

The good: This food is low in Saturated Fat, and very low in Cholesterol. It is also a good source of Vitamin A, Calcium, Iron, Magnesium and Phosphorus, and a very good source of Dietary Fiber, Vitamin C, Vitamin K and Potassium.

The bad: This food is high in Sodium, and a large portion of the calories in this food come from sugars.
